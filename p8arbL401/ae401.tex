	{\bf Work, energy and performance }\\
	\begin{enumerate}[1.]
		%Task 1
		{\bf \item Task}
		\begin{enumerate}[a)]
			\item Briefly describe the difference between work and performance.
			\item Briefly justify the following statement: "If two people do the same work, then they don't necessarily have to do the same thing."
			\item How can one determine whether a person A (in the physical sense)
			does more than person B?
		\end{enumerate}
		
		{\item \bf A car has the power $P=\SI{150}{\kilo\watt}$. - What energy can this engine deliver in one hour?}
		
		{\bf \item Task }
		\begin{enumerate}[a)]
			\item Explain the following statements:
			\begin{itemize}
				\item "The efficiency of a real machine is always less than 1."
				\item "A car has an efficiency of $\eta = 40 \%$."
			\end{itemize}
			\item A crane needs an energy of about $\SI{25}{\kilo\joule}$ to lift a circular saw ($m=\SI{90}{\kilogram}$) to a height of $\SI{25}{\meter}$. \\
			What is the efficiency of this crane?
			\item The saw hangs on the crane (usually) over the weekend. - How much work does the crane do during this time? (Short justification).
		\end{enumerate}
		
		
		
		{\bf \item To what height was a pallet with beverage crates with the weight of $F_G=\SI{1500}{\newton}$
			lifted,} if work of $W=\SI{12}{\kilo\joule}$ was done on it?
		
		{\bf \item In a "sun farm", solar modules are installed} in such a way that they are always aligned perpendicular to the sun's rays over the course of a day. In the Sahara, an average radiation output of $ \SI{1}{\kilo\watt\over{\meter^2}} $ is achieved for 12 hours.  on the modules.
			
			\begin{enumerate}
				\item How much energy can be obtained from one square meter of module area during this time?
				\item How much energy is gained if the efficiency is only \\
				$\eta = 17.5\%$?
				\item How large would the area of such a sun farm have to be if \\
				$\SI{6e10}{\joule}$ of daily energy should be gained?
			\end{enumerate}
			
			
			{\bf \item A motor pump with $P=\SI{2.0}{\kilo\watt}$ output pumps 5000 liters of water from a well shaft in $t= \SI{4.0}{\minute}$}
			\begin{enumerate}[a)]
				\item What type of mechanical work does the pump perform?
				\item How deep is the shaft?
			\end{enumerate}
		\end{enumerate}
