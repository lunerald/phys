	\begin{minipage}{0.3\textwidth}
		The picture shows how hydraulics work.
		The work $$W=F\cdot s$$ is the same on both sides,
		likewise the pressure $$p={F \over A}$$ is the same everywhere.
		Furthermore:
		$${F_2 \over F_1}={A_2 \over A_1}={s_1 \over s_2}$$
	\end{minipage}
